Stefán Ólafsson

Assistant Professor · Reykjavik University

I am an Assistant Professor at Reykjavik University. My research lies at the intersection of human-computer interaction, natural language processing, and health informatics. I design and study conversational agents and virtual agents that support health behavior change, clinical assessment, and patient safety. Previously, I completed my PhD at Northeastern University working with the Relational Agents Group.

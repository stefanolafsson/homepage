Consulting Services

Drawing on years of academic research and applied work in AI, natural language processing, and health technology, I offer consulting to organizations looking to design, build, or evaluate intelligent systems. Whether you are exploring conversational AI for the first time or refining an existing product, I can help you navigate the landscape with a research-grounded perspective.

Conversational AI & Virtual Agents

Design and evaluation of dialogue systems, chatbots, and embodied virtual agents. Expertise in task-oriented dialogue, persuasive agents, and user engagement strategies.

Natural Language Processing

Text understanding, information extraction, and language model integration. Guidance on selecting, fine-tuning, and deploying NLP models for your domain.

Digital health interventions, patient-facing technology, mobile health applications, and safety considerations for health-related AI systems.

Risk assessment of conversational AI systems, bias auditing, and designing safeguards for sensitive or high-stakes applications.
